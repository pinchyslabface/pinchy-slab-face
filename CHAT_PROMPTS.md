## Market Research Chat

Use this prompt:

> We’re working on PSF as an indoor climbing audience and gym intelligence project, starting in Australia. Please help size the Australian indoor climbing and bouldering market for decision-making, not for generic desk research. Read `MASTER_PLAN.md`, `POSITIONING.md`, and `README.md` first. Then produce a sourced market brief that separates facts from inference and covers: total participation in indoor climbing, bouldering, and adjacent climbing categories; state-by-state demand where available; core demographics; spend and pricing signals; number of climbing gyms in Australia with a full gym list by state; major chains and independents; city concentration; growth signals since the Tokyo and Paris Olympics; and implications for PSF as a newsletter and audience business. Prioritise Australian Sports Commission / AusPlay, ABS, state and national climbing bodies, gym websites, and high-quality industry sources. Where exact figures are unavailable, estimate carefully, explain the method, and show confidence levels.
