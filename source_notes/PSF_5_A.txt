# Indoor Climbing Events Platform – Production-Ready Spec (v2)

---

## 1. Project Overview

A centralized platform for discovering indoor climbing gym events across Australia.

**Mission:**  
Make it effortless for climbers to **discover relevant events in their city** and for gyms to **increase event participation**.

**Core Insight:**  
Event information is fragmented across gym websites and social media (primarily Instagram). There is no single, reliable source of truth.

**Approach:**  
Start as a **lean aggregation + distribution engine** (social + email), build an audience, then evolve into a structured platform.

---

## 2. Core Product vs Supporting Features

### 🟢 Core Product (Non-Negotiable)

**Value Proposition:**  
> “Never miss a climbing event in your city.”

**Core Functions:**
- Aggregate events from all gyms
- Normalize and structure event data
- Distribute via:
  - Instagram (discovery)
  - Email (retention, segmentation)

**Primary Users:**
- Climbers (end users)
- Gyms (event providers)

---

### 🟡 Supporting Features (Growth & Engagement)

These accelerate growth but are not the product itself:

- Monthly giveaways
- User-generated content (UGC)
- Event recaps / highlights
- Influencer / climber amplification
- Hashtag strategy
- Gym spotlights
- Community reposts

---

## 3. MVP Scope (Phase 1)

### Goal

Validate:
- Demand for centralized event discovery
- Ability to grow audience
- Gym willingness to participate

---

### Included

- Instagram account (primary channel)
- Landing page (email capture)
- Email newsletter (state segmented)
- Google Form (event submission)
- Google Sheets (event database)
- Manual + semi-automated aggregation

---

### Excluded

- Full website
- User accounts
- Advanced filtering/search
- Mobile app
- Monetization features
