# Project: Pinchy Slab Face (Global Climbing Hub)
**Date:** April 2026
**Status:** Discovery / Strategy Phase

---

## 1. Executive Summary
A global-first climbing directory and "Discovery Pass" system. The project solves fragmentation in the climbing community by centralizing events and gym data, monetized through a seasonal pass model and a private-label gear brand (liquid chalk/tape).

## 2. Core Product Pillars
* **The Global Directory:** Multi-site architecture (`/uk`, `/us`, `/au`) using a rigid hierarchy: Continent > Country > State > City.
* **The Discovery Pass:** A time-bound pass (e.g., "The London Summer Pass") where users visit 10 gyms for a set fee.
* **The Prize Pool:** Sponsor-funded rewards. A "Cash Flow" model where revenue is collected upfront and prizes are distributed at the end of the season.
* **Private Label Flywheel:** Using the prize pool to seed a physical brand (Scented Liquid Chalk, Boar’s Hair Brushes). Winners get samples -> QR codes drive recurring full-price sales.

## 3. Competitive Landscape (UK/US Focus)
* **The Gap:** Most competitors are either broad "activity finders" or localized blogs. 
* **Our Edge:** Hyper-focus on "What's On" (events/comps) rather than just static gym addresses. Using AI scraping to maintain real-time accuracy.

## 4. Technical Architecture (The "Codex" Blueprint)
* **Framework:** Headless CMS (WordPress/Bricks) or Next.js for SEO performance.
* **Data Scrapers:** Automated monitoring of gym Instagram/Websites via tools like Browse.ai or custom Python scripts.
* **User Flow:** 1. Purchase Pass -> 2. Digital Logbook (Check-in via Geo-fencing or QR) -> 3. Milestone Rewards (3, 5, 10 gyms) -> 4. Physical Product Fulfillment.
* **Database Schema:**
    - `Gym_Profile`: Location, Wall Height, Bouldering/Lead, Pricing, Event_Feed.
    - `User_Pass`: Activation_Date, Gyms_Visited_Array, Reward_Tier_Status.

## 5. Monetization & Growth
* **Breakage Revenue:** Profit generated from users who buy the pass but don't visit all 10 gyms.
* **Sponsorship:** Charging brands for "Volume of Exposure" (e.g., "This milestone brought to you by [Brand]").
* **Growth Hack:** "Squad Packs" for University clubs and dual-sided referral entries for the Grand Prize.

## 6. Immediate Action Items
1.  **Alpha Launch:** Select one high-density climbing city (e.g., London or Melbourne).
2.  **Web-Scraper Setup:** Begin aggregating the "What's On" data for the pilot city.
3.  **Pitch Deck:** Formalize the "Discovery Pass" logic for gym owners (demonstrating new foot traffic).
