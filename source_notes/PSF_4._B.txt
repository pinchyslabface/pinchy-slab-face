# STRATEGY.md — Indoor Climbing Events Platform

---

## 🧭 Strategic Overview

This project is a **niche media + marketplace foundation**, not just a product.

The goal is to build:
> A **highly targeted, deeply segmented audience of indoor climbers**, monetised through sponsorships, partnerships, and eventually marketplace dynamics.

---

## 🎯 Core Strategic Insight

> **The email list is the asset. Not the website.**

Everything else (site, socials, tools) exists to:
- Grow the list
- Improve segmentation
- Increase engagement
- Increase monetisation potential

---

## 💰 Monetisation Strategy (Deep Dive)

### Primary Revenue: Newsletter Sponsorships

#### 📊 CPM Benchmarks

| Audience Type | CPM Range |
|--------------|----------|
| General | $10–$30 |
| Niche (climbing) | $30–$75 |
| Highly targeted (segmented) | $75–$150+ |

---

### 💵 Revenue Scenarios

#### 10,000 Subscribers
- $30 CPM → $300 per email
- $50 CPM → $500 per email
- Monthly (4 sends): $1,200–$2,000

#### 20,000 Subscribers
- $600–$1,500 per email
- Monthly: $2,400–$6,000

#### 30,000 Subscribers
- $900–$2,250 per email
- Monthly: $3,600–$9,000+

---

### 🧩 Sponsorship Structure

#### Recommended Model

**Phase 1 (0–10k subs)**
- 1 sponsor per email
- Flat fee or early CPM

**Phase 2 (10k–30k)**
- 1 Primary sponsor
- 1 Secondary sponsor

**Phase 3 (30k+)**
- Still max 2 sponsors
- Increase pricing via segmentation

---

### 📍 Placement Hierarchy

1. **Header / Hero Sponsor**
   - Highest value
   - Premium pricing

2. **Native Mid-Content**
   - High engagement
   - Feels organic

3. **Footer**
   - Lower value
   - Secondary sponsors only

---

### 📦 Packaging Strategy

Instead of selling single placements:

#### Monthly Packages
- 4 emails (weekly)
- Instagram mentions
- Optional website placement

Example:
- Bronze: Footer placements
- Silver: Mid-email + social
- Gold: Exclusive + top placement

---

### 🔒 Premium Levers (Increase CPM)

You can charge more when you offer:

- Geo targeting (e.g. Melbourne climbers only)
- Skill segmentation (beginner vs advanced)
- Event-specific targeting
- Category exclusivity (only 1 shoe brand)

---

## 📈 Growth Strategy

### Phase 1: Scrappy Growth (0–1k subs)

#### Channels
- Instagram (primary)
- Gym partnerships
- Direct outreach
- Local climbing groups

#### Tactics
- DM gyms: “We’ll promote your events for free”
- Tag gyms in every post
- Encourage resharing

---

### Phase 2: Momentum (1k–10k subs)

- Monthly giveaways
- Collaborations with gyms
- Feature climbers with social following
- Referral loops (share to friends)

---

### Phase 3: Scale (10k+ subs)

- Paid ads (Instagram)
- SEO via website
- Partnerships with brands
- Expansion to other cities

---

## 🧠 Content Strategy

### 🎯 Core Principle
> Content should make people feel like they’re **“in the loop” locally**

---

### Weekly Email Structure (Optimised)

1. **Hero Section**
   - Sponsor (or featured event)

2. **🔥 Featured Events**
   - 2–3 standout events

3. **📅 This Week (By Day)**
   - Mon–Sun breakdown
   - Clean + scannable

4. **📍 By Area (Optional)**
   - If density increases

5. **🧩 Engagement Block (rotate weekly)**
   - Climber spotlight
   - Gym spotlight
   - Training tip
   - Community update

---

### 🧲 High-Engagement Content Ideas

#### 1. Climber Spotlights
- Feature local climbers
- Prefer those with social following
- Drives sharing + growth

---

#### 2. Gym / Route Setter Spotlights
- Builds relationships
- Encourages gyms to promote you

---

#### 3. “What’s New” at Gyms
- New routes
- New facilities
- Changes

---

#### 4. Competitions & Results
- Who won comps
- Rankings
- Photos

---

#### 5. Training Tips
- Short, actionable
- Builds habit

---

#### 6. Community Content
- Photo of the week
- User submissions
- Stories

---

#### 7. Giveaways
- Gear, passes
- Drives viral growth

---

## 🔁 Growth Loops

### Loop 1: Gym Amplification
1. You feature gym
2. Gym shares post/email
3. Their audience subscribes
4. More events → more value

---

### Loop 2: Climber Spotlight
1. Feature climber
2. Climber shares
3. Followers join
4. Repeat

---

### Loop 3: Giveaways
1. Offer prize
2. Require share/follow
3. Grow list + socials

## 🧠 Segmentation Strategy (Key Asset)

### Data You Want Over Time
- Location (state/city)
- Skill level
- Experience (years climbing)
- Age bracket
- Individual vs family

---

### Why This Matters
- Higher CPMs
- Better sponsor targeting
- More relevant content
- Higher engagement

---

## ⚠️ Strategic Risks

### 1. Supply Risk
- Not enough events to sustain weekly emails

**Mitigation:**
- Include broader content (news, tips, features)

---

### 2. Engagement Risk
- Emails become repetitive

**Mitigation:**
- Rotate content blocks
- Add human stories

---

### 3. Gym Dependency
- Relying on gyms for data

**Mitigation:**
- Build direct audience relationship

---

### 4. Overbuilding Too Early
- Building website before validation

**Mitigation:**
- Stay email-first
