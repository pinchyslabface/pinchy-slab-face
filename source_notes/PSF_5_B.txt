
---

### Components

#### 1. Data Input

- Google Form (gym submissions)
- Manual entry (admin)
- Optional: Instagram scraping (Python)

---

#### 2. Data Storage

- Google Sheets (single source of truth)

Tabs:
- Events
- Gyms
- Subscribers (optional mirror)

---

#### 3. Processing Layer (Manual + Light Automation)

- Review submissions
- Clean/standardize data
- Tag by state/city
- Queue for publishing

---

#### 4. Distribution

- Instagram posts
- Instagram stories
- Email newsletters (segmented)
