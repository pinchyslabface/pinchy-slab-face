# Indoor Climbing Events Platform – MVP Project Document

---

## 🧭 Project Overview

Build a **centralised discovery platform for indoor climbing events** (comps, socials, training nights), starting with a **content-first MVP (email newsletter + landing page)** before expanding into a full website.

### Core Hypothesis
Climbers want a **single, reliable source** to discover local climbing events, and gyms are willing to **contribute and promote** that platform.

---

## 🎯 Core Product vs Secondary Features

### ✅ Core Product (Primary Value Driver)
- Weekly **“What’s On” email newsletter**
- Curated list of **climbing events by city/state**
- Simple **event discovery**
- Email list with **rich user segmentation (state, skill, etc.)**
- **Audience monetisation via sponsors**

👉 This is the product. Everything else supports it.

---

### ⚙️ Supporting Features (Growth / Engagement)
- Landing page with email capture
- Instagram/social presence
- Event submission form
- Climber/gym spotlights
- Training tips / community content
- Giveaways
- Website event archive (later)

---

## 🚀 MVP Scope (Phase 1)

### 🎯 Goal
Validate demand before building a full platform.

### Components

#### 1. Weekly Email Newsletter
- Main distribution channel
- Format:
  - Featured events (top 3–5)
  - Events by **day of week**
  - Grouped by **city/state**
  - Links to original event pages
- Optional sections:
  - Featured gym/event
  - Climber spotlight
  - Quick community update

---

#### 2. Landing Page
- Purpose: **capture emails**
- Content:
  - Clear value prop
  - Signup form (with segmentation)
  - Social proof (gyms/events included)
- Tools:
  - Carrd or WordPress

---

#### 3. Email Capture + Segmentation
- Fields:
  - Email
  - State / City
  - Optional: skill level, experience, etc.
- Enables:
  - Region-specific emails
  - Future targeting for sponsors

---

#### 4. Social Presence (Instagram-first)
- Promote events
- Tag gyms/climbers
- Drive email signups
- Encourage sharing

---

#### 5. Event Collection (Manual)
- Sources:
  - Gym Instagram pages
  - Gym websites
  - Direct outreach
  - Submission form
- No automation initially

---

## 🌐 Phase 2: MVP Website (Post-Traction)

Only build once email shows traction.

### Features
- Event listing (by state/city)
- “This week” / “This month” filters
- Event submission form
- SEO pages (e.g. “Climbing events Melbourne”)
