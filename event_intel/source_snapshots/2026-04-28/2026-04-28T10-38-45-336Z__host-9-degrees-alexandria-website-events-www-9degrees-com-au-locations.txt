Chatswood
Monday 6am - 10pm Tuesday ** 4pm - 10pm Wednesday 6am - 10pm Thursday 10am - 10pm Friday 10am - 10pm Weekends 8am - 10pm
** From Tue 13th of Jan our upstairs training area is open from 10am on Tuesdays. Please note that the main climbing area is out of bounds until 4pm while our route setters put up new climbs. All visitors are limited to board climbing and weight training during this time. Due to the advanced nature of board climbing we would recommend waiting until 4pm to visit if you’re newer to climbing.
