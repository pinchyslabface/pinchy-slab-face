Directions

Driving:

Enter the industrial estate off Unwins Bridge Rd, St Peters – nearest corner is Bedwin Rd. Plenty of parking, either in the front carpark, or drive down the left side to the huge rear carpark. Please note the signage in the car parks, thanks.

Train:

Nearest stations are St Peters (closest) or Sydenham Stations, both about a 10 min walk.

Bus:

Route 422, nearest stop is on The Princes Hwy, near Victoria St, 5 min walk away.

Cycle:

Bike racks available.

About SICG St Peters

The Southern Hemisphere’s biggest climbing gym, with 3700m2 of climbing walls.

We are Sydney’s only climbing gym with BOTH world class climbing and bouldering.

We’ve been the centre of Sydney’s biggest climbing community since 1993, continually expanding and improving to the world class facility that we have today. The only Aussie climbing gym to host a world championship level comp, the 2008 World Youth Championships.

For Kids Club (Stone Monkeys), Tuition including lead climbing courses and School Holidays Rockcamp, go to: www.sydneyrockclimbing.com.au
