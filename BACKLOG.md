# Backlog

These ideas are worth keeping, but they are not the first thing to execute.

## 1. Climbing Directory

Purpose:

- structured gym listings
- event discovery
- SEO pages by city and state

Why it stays later:

- it depends on frequent data maintenance
- it is useful, but the audience list is more important first

## 2. City Pass Products

Examples:

- discovery pass
- city-wide gym pass
- seasonal challenge pass

Why it stays later:

- requires audience, gym relationships, and operational coordination

## 3. 3D-Printed Accessories

Examples:

- brush holders
- shoe deodoriser inserts
- small organisers
- portable grip tools that are non-load-bearing

Why it stays later:

- worth revisiting once the audience exists
- strong fit with your fabrication capability

## 4. Clothing and Merch

Examples:

- Pinchy Slab Face merch
- climbing-inspired streetwear
- chalk bag or bucket adjacent apparel

Why it stays later:

- branding becomes much easier once the audience is real
- fashion usually works better with proof of demand

## 5. Training Boards and Larger Hardware

Why it stays later:

- higher design and liability stakes
- should not distract from the first revenue engine

## Backlog Rule

If we cannot explain why an idea helps the email list, it stays in the backlog for now.
