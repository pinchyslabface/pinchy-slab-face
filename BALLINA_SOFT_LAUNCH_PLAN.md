# Ballina Soft Launch Plan

## Decision

Use Ballina as a soft MVP launch, not a full product launch.

The immediate goal is to collect qualified early subscribers from the Youth Nationals / state team cohort and learn where the strongest next signal is.

## Must-Have Assets

### 1. Beehiiv Landing Page

Purpose:

- collect email signups
- explain PSF in one sentence
- make the product feel useful before the first full issue exists

Working headline:

- Pinchy Slab Face is coming soon

Working subhead:

- The indoor climbing scene email for comps, gyms, events, and community updates.

State-team-cohort angle:

- Join early and help shape what climbers, parents, coaches, and supporters want from PSF.

Required field:

- email

Optional fields if fast to configure:

- home state or region
- role: climber, parent, coach, setter, gym, brand, supporter

Confirmation message:

- You're in. We'll send a short welcome note and let you know when PSF goes live.

### 2. QR Flyer Or Card

Purpose:

- make the pitch easy to understand in under ten seconds
- convert real-world conversations into signups

Front copy:

- Pinchy Slab Face
- Indoor climbing news, comps, events, and community updates.
- Coming soon.
- Scan to join early.

Optional small line:

- Built for climbers, parents, coaches, gyms, and the people who keep the scene moving.

Back or footer:

- First emails coming soon.
- Join early and help shape what PSF covers.

### 3. Welcome Email

Subject:

- You're on the Pinchy Slab Face list

Body shape:

- thanks for joining
- PSF is a coming-soon indoor climbing scene email
- it will cover comps, events, gyms, and useful community updates
- ask them to reply with what they most want included
- mention that early Ballina signups will help shape the first version

One-question reply prompt:

- What would make this email genuinely useful for you or your climber?

## Nice-To-Have Only

Do these only if they do not delay the must-have assets:

- giveaway or comp
- referral prompt
- sponsor logo or prize mention
- Instagram launch post
- extra profile survey
- gym re-share pack

## Tracking

Use one QR destination for Ballina.

If practical, add source tracking to the URL:

- source: ballina
- medium: qr
- campaign: soft_launch

After Ballina, review:

- total signups
- signup conversion if scan data is available
- state or region split
- role split if collected
- replies to the welcome email
- verbal feedback from conversations

## Handout Script

"We're starting Pinchy Slab Face, a coming-soon indoor climbing scene email for comps, gyms, events, and community updates. We're collecting early signups here at Ballina so climbers, parents, coaches, and supporters can help shape what it becomes."

## Post-Trip Decision

After the trip, decide:

- which state or city showed the strongest pull
- whether Melbourne remains the first full newsletter market
- whether state-team and comp families deserve their own early segment
- what the first real issue should include
