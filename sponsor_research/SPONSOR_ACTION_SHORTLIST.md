# Sponsor Action Shortlist

This is the most operational sponsor view in the current system.

It groups the best targets by the actual sponsorship ask rather than by generic sponsor quality.

## 1. Launch Giveaway Product

Best first targets:

- `Rhino Skin Solutions`
- `Climbskin`
- `Friction Labs`
- `Chalk Cartel`
- `Reset`
- `Awesome Woodys`
- `Crux Gear`
- `Climbing Anchors`

Why these win:

- high perceived value
- easy pack inclusion
- strong fit for early launch momentum
- easiest path to getting real sponsor participation before scale is fully proven

## 2. Street Team / Sponsored Climber Product Support

Best first targets:

- `Scarpa Australia`
- `La Sportiva Australia`
- `Rhino Skin Solutions`
- `Friction Labs`
- `Chalk Cartel`
- `Reset`
- `Awesome Woodys`
- `Lattice Training`
- `Unparallel`
- `Evolv`

Why these win:

- strong youth-climber relevance
- good repeat-use product categories
- plausible support for selected climbers rather than huge product volume
- strong social proof potential when tied to Ballina and youth nationals

## 3. PSF Merch / Package Support

Best first targets:

- `Climbing Anchors`
- `Paddy Pallin`
- `K2 Base Camp`
- `Happy Biner`
- `The Climbing Shop`
- `Pinnacle Sports`
- `Friction Addiction`
- `Mammut`

Why these win:

- strong product-access logic
- better fit for bundles vouchers accessory support or package economics
- more realistic than expecting every endemic brand to help with physical package logistics

## 4. Long-Term Paid Sponsors

Best first targets:

- `La Sportiva Australia`
- `Scarpa Australia`
- `Black Diamond`
- `Lattice Training`
- `Rhino Skin Solutions`
- `Climbing Anchors`

## 5. Foundational Multi-Angle Sponsors

These are the names most likely to matter across more than one ask:

- `La Sportiva Australia`
- `Scarpa Australia`
- `Black Diamond`
- `Lattice Training`
- `Rhino Skin Solutions`
- `Climbing Anchors`

These matter most because they can plausibly support:

- launch credibility
- youth or street-team story
- product contribution
- longer-term sponsor value

## Recommended Use

- If the ask is easiest early traction: start with `Launch Giveaway Product`.
- If the goal is youth or athlete identity: start with `Street Team / Sponsored Climber Product Support`.
- If the goal is making physical packs and PSF merch viable: start with `PSF Merch / Package Support`.
- If the goal is durable revenue: focus on `Long-Term Paid Sponsors`.
- If time is limited: start with `Foundational Multi-Angle Sponsors`.
